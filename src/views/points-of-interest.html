Points of interest

Old Town Square

It is the heart of the oldest district of Prague and the most emblematic meeting point or starting point of a visit to the city.

Astronomical Clock

Located in the Old Town Square, it is a unique work of art of its kind. Every hour on the hour, it comes to life with the parade of the figures of the 12 apostles.

Charles Bridge

This iconic bridge spans the Vltava River and connects Stare Mesto (Old Town) with Mala Strana (Lesser Town). It is adorned with 30 statues on both sides

Prague Castle

It is a former royal citadel that has been the office of the kings of Bohemia, the emperors of the Holy Roman Empire, the presidents of Czechoslovakia and the presidents of the Czech Republic

St. Vitus Cathedral

St. Vitus Cathedral: It is an impressive Gothic cathedral located inside the Prague Castle.

Vyšehrad

Vyšehrad: It is a riverside citadel offering panoramic views of the city.
